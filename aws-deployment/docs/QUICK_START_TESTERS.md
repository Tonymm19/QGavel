## ğŸš€ Getting Started (5 Steps)

### Step 1: Log In
1. Go to https://qgavel.com
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Step 2: Explore the Dashboard
After logging in, you'll see:
- **Sidebar** (left) - Navigate to Judges, Cases, Deadlines
- **Main Area** - Upcoming deadlines and recent cases
- **Your Name** (top right) - Settings and logout

### Step 3: Browse Judges
1. Click **"Judges"** in the sidebar
2. See the list of N.D. Illinois judges
3. Click any judge to see their profile and procedures

### Step 4: Create a Test Case
1. Click **"Cases"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"+ New Case"**
3. Fill in:
   - Case Number: `1:24-cv-TEST01`
   - Caption: `Test v. Testing`
   - Select a judge
4. Click **"Create Case"**

### Step 5: Add a Deadline
1. Open your test case
2. Click **"+ Add Deadline"**
3. Fill in:
   - Title: `Test Deadline`
   - Due Date: Pick a date next week
4. Click **"Create Deadline"**
