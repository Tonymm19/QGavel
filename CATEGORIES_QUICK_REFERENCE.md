# MOST COMMON CATEGORIES (Cross-Referenced)

## 1. TIME-RELATED
- **Days before/after events**: 3, 7, 14, 21, 28, 30, 60, 90, 120 days
- **Months**: 3, 9, 12-15 months
- **Hours**: 24 hours, midnight deadline

## 2. SIZE/LENGTH-RELATED
- **Page limits**: 3, 5, 7, 8, 10, 15, 20, 25 pages
- **File size**: 50 MB maximum
- **Numbered items**: 40, 80 paragraphs

## 3. FORMAT-RELATED
- **File type**: PDF, text-searchable
- **Binding**: Binder clips only
- **Spacing**: Double-spaced vs single-spaced (implied)
- **Structure**: Separately numbered paragraphs

## 4. PROCEDURAL-RELATED
- **Conferral**: Good faith meet and confer
- **Certification**: Certificate of compliance
- **Authority**: Leave of court, good cause shown
- **Service**: Must serve all parties

# CATEGORY PATTERNS BY DOCUMENT TYPE

## Motion-Related
- Page limits (7-15 pages typical)
- Response timeframes (21 days typical)
- Reply timeframes (14 days typical)
- Content requirements (issues, facts, argument)

## Discovery-Related
- Meet and confer requirement
- Timeframes (30-90 days)
- Format requirements (quote in full)
- Sanctions for non-compliance

## Expert-Related
- Report deadlines (30 days typical)
- Content requirements (methodology, data, prior testimony)
- Page limits (none to 25 pages)
- Daubert motion procedures

## Trial-Related
- Pretrial conference (21 days before)
- Jury instructions (14 days before pretrial)
- Motions in limine (28 days before)
- Trial briefs (7 days before)
