# KEY OBSERVATIONS

1. **Timeframes/Deadlines** are the most prevalent category across both court rules and judge procedures
2. **Page limits** are highly specific to individual judges
3. **Document format requirements** vary between general court rules and specific judge preferences
4. **Content requirements** are detailed and must include specific elements
5. **Procedural prerequisites** (like meet and confer requirements) are mandatory before certain actions
6. **Strict enforcement** language appears frequently, indicating these are not mere suggestions
